George Brown College (GBC) is a public college located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada 
and was established in 1967. 
It offers a wide range of programs across various fields, 
including health sciences, business, arts and design, community services, and technology.
The college has three main campuses: St. James (downtown Toronto), Casa Loma, and Waterfront 
and it serves over 30,000 full-time students and 30,000 part-time students.
GBC is known for its hands-on learning approach, 
preparing students for the workforce with practical skills and industry connections.
